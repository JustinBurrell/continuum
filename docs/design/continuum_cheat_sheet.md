# Continuum Design Cheat Sheet
**Quick Reference for Figma Design | Version 1.0**

---

## Primary Brand Colors

### Purple (Primary Brand Color)
**Hex:** `#6B21A8`  
**RGB:** `107, 33, 168`  
**Use for:** Primary buttons, links, active states, brand elements, CTAs

### Background Gray
**Hex:** `#F8F9FA`  
**RGB:** `248, 249, 250`  
**Use for:** Main app background, page backgrounds, space between cards

---

## Semantic Colors

### Success Green
**Hex:** `#059669`  
**RGB:** `5, 150, 105`  
**Use for:** Success messages, completed tasks, positive feedback, checkmarks

### Warning Orange
**Hex:** `#EA580C`  
**RGB:** `234, 88, 12`  
**Use for:** Warning messages, upcoming deadlines, caution indicators

### Error Red
**Hex:** `#DC2626`  
**RGB:** `220, 38, 38`  
**Use for:** Error messages, failed actions, overdue tasks, destructive actions

---

## Neutral Colors

### Text Colors
- **Primary Text:** `#111827` (Gray 900) - Headings, body text
- **Secondary Text:** `#6B7280` (Gray 500) - Supporting text, metadata
- **Disabled Text:** `#9CA3AF` (Gray 400) - Disabled states

### UI Colors
- **White Surface:** `#FFFFFF` - Cards, modals, dropdowns
- **Border:** `#D1D5DB` (Gray 300) - Borders, dividers, separators
- **Hover Background:** `#F3F4F6` (Gray 100) - Hover states on white surfaces

---

## Color Usage Guidelines

### Buttons
- **Primary Button:** `#6B21A8` background with white text
- **Secondary Button:** White background with `#6B21A8` border and text
- **Success Button:** `#059669` background with white text
- **Danger Button:** `#DC2626` background with white text

### Status Badges
- **Success:** `#059669` text on `#D1FAE5` background
- **Warning:** `#EA580C` text on `#FFEDD5` background
- **Error:** `#DC2626` text on `#FEE2E2` background
- **Info:** `#6B21A8` text on `#EDE9FE` background

### Form Elements
- **Input Background:** `#FFFFFF`
- **Input Border (default):** `#D1D5DB`
- **Input Border (focus):** `#6B21A8`
- **Placeholder Text:** `#9CA3AF`

### Navigation
- **Active Nav Item:** `#6B21A8`
- **Inactive Nav Item:** `#6B7280`
- **Nav Background:** `#FFFFFF` or `#F8F9FA`

---

## Design Principles

### 1. Clarity Over Cleverness
Prioritize clear communication and intuitive interactions over decorative elements.

### 2. Consistency Builds Trust
Use established patterns, colors, and spacing throughout the app.

### 3. Progressive Disclosure
Show essential information first, reveal complexity as needed.

### 4. Responsive & Adaptive
Design for mobile-first, scale up gracefully to larger screens.

### 5. Accessible by Default
Every design decision should consider all users, including those with disabilities.
